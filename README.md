# Mark Adjustments Dashboard (2025)

Mark adjustments are required when a student's final mark needs to be manually amended on the university system due to administrative, academic, or system-related reasons. To improve monitoring and reporting, I developed a Power BI dashboard that tracks all mark adjustment requests submitted during 2025.

Dashboard Highlights

Monthly trend analysis of mark adjustment requests to identify peak processing periods.
Faculty-level comparison of submissions to highlight workload distribution across the institution.
Request status monitoring through a slicer that identifies requests referred back due to incomplete forms or missing supporting documentation.
Process compliance reporting to identify faculties that may require additional training or support.

Key Insights

Submission volumes peaked during February and December.
Arts and Humanities submitted the highest number of requests.
Referred-back requests provide an indication of process compliance and training needs.
Trend analysis supports workload planning and resource allocation.

# Remark Processing Dashboard

Students may apply for a remark of an examination paper if they believe their assessment result does not accurately reflect their performance. Monitoring the remark process is important, as prolonged turnaround times can impact students' academic planning and progression.

To improve visibility into the process, I developed a Power BI dashboard that measures the average response time at each stage of the remark workflow, from student application through to the final outcome.

Dashboard Highlights

Tracks average response times for students, departments, examiners, and faculty/liaison offices.
Identifies stages within the process that contribute most significantly to delays.
Includes a slicer to compare remark requests that required a mark adjustment against those that did not.
Supports process improvement initiatives by highlighting bottlenecks and turnaround times.

Key Insights

Departments and faculty offices account for the longest response times in the process.
Remark requests that result in a mark adjustment generally take longer to finalize.
The dashboard enables stakeholders to identify bottlenecks and monitor service delivery performance.
Response time analysis supports efforts to improve turnaround times and enhance the student experience.
